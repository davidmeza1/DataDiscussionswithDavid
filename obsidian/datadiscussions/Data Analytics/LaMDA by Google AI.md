### LaMDA by Google AI
 a language model trained for dialog applications. It is pre-trained on ~3 B documents and ~1 B dialogs and fine-tuned on human generated data to improve on quality, safety and truthfulness of the generated text. It is also fine-tuned to learn to call an external information retrieval system such as Google Search, a calculator, and a translator making it potentially a much stronger candidate to replace Google Search than ChatGPT.
